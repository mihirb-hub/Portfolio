Formula Slug (Formula SAE)

PCB Designer

EV Charging & PCBA Design

I designed and built a custom PCB to regulate J1772 negotiation using RC filters and linear regulators to generate different voltages. This board powers an STM32 microcontroller and drives power and RGB signals to a screen IC, which sends live charging data through a CAN bus directly to the display. The entire system was wired into our custom charging enclosure, safely taking a J1772 input to charge the vehicle's tractive battery.

Hardware Integration & Bring-up
